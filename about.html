Who are we?

We are two guys who wanted to change an industry. Starting at our humble beginnings in Remington, Indiana we have always had a passion for cooking and for business. Both of us attended Kelley School of Business through Indiana University in Indianapolis and despite beginning our careers as businessmen, we both knew we wanted to get back into food. In 2017 we decided to start small with a booth at the Marion County Fair. When we had great success there we decided to open a small storefront in northern Indianapolis. We realized that we were not totally passionate about the restaurant scene so in January 2018 we bought a food truck. We continue our work at businesses and festivals all around Indianapolis and we love changing the way people view food trucks. Our goal by 2020 is to buy a second truck for Indianapolis, by 2025 to be in Cincinnati, by 2030 to be in Chicago. We are dreaming big and we are confident we can accomplish anything.
